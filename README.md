## 📌 What is ALP Astrology?

**ALP (Akshaya Lagna Paddhati) Astrology** is an advanced astrological method founded by **Dr. S. Pothuvudai Moorthy**.

Unlike traditional astrology systems that rely mainly on **Dasa/Bukthi (Moon-based timing)**, ALP Astrology focuses on the **continuous movement of the Ascendant (Lagna)** over time.

👉 In ALP:

* The **Lagna moves 1 Nakshatra Pada every 1 year, 1 month, and 10 days**
* It completes one full zodiac cycle in **120 years**

This dynamic tracking allows **precise event timing** in a person’s life.

---

## 🧠 Core Concept

ALP Astrology is based on the principle that:

> Just like the Moon moves across the zodiac, the **Lagna also progresses continuously over time**, influencing life events.

This makes ALP a **time-sensitive predictive system**, not just a static birth-chart analysis.
